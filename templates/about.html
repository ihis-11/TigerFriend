About the App

TigerFriend is a web app that anonymously connects Princeton students all throughout campus over
their shared interests and activities.

Users sign up on the platform and fill out a questionnaire to get matched with other users based on
similar responses in different categories. From there, users have free reign to message their matches through a
built-in chat system that guarantees their anonymity.
